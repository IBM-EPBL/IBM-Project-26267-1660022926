TIMETABLE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Timetable management system is a vital tool in administrative efficiency and efforts. In an institution, where scheduling is a necessary task for smooth functioning, a timetable management system will aid in the planning of proper schedules for faculty and students. An effective timetable management requires convenience in scheduling hours and absence of scheduling conflicts. Some factors to be considered are that no two faculty members are assigned to the same class; no two classes are assigned the same faculty member and in cases where the faculty member has taken a leave of absence there should be another member assigned. The proposed system will help tackle the problems that come under scheduling. The Timetable Management System is a web service with functions that will support login, class allotment, and generation and planning of the respective timetables.
